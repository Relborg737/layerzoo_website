# LayerZoo FAQ

## What is LayerZoo?
LayerZoo is an educational resource focused on Bitcoin and its Layer 2 ecosystem. It provides tools, decision trees, and collections to help educators and learners understand scaling solutions built on Bitcoin.

## What are Bitcoin Layer 2s?
Layer 2 solutions enhance Bitcoin's base layer by enabling faster, cheaper transactions while inheriting Bitcoin's security. Examples include sidechains, rollups, and state channels. They allow for broader adoption in education, DeFi, and everyday use without compromising the core principles of Bitcoin.

## What is the difference between a Coin and a Token?
A coin is native to its own blockchain (e.g., STX on Stacks, which uses Proof of Transfer). A token exists on top of another blockchain, often issued via smart contracts (e.g., tokens on Stacks or other layers).

## Key Layer 2 Projects
### Ark
Ark provides a Layer 2 protocol for Bitcoin focused on improving liquidity and transaction efficiency through virtual UTXOs and off-chain coordination.

### Citrea
Citrea is a zero-knowledge rollup on Bitcoin, bringing EVM compatibility and smart contract functionality while settling on the Bitcoin mainnet for security.

### Rootstock (RSK)
Rootstock is a sidechain that enables smart contracts on Bitcoin with merged mining for shared security. It supports RBTC, a Bitcoin-pegged token.

### Stacks
Stacks brings smart contracts and decentralized applications to Bitcoin using its unique Proof of Transfer (PoX) consensus, allowing BTC holders to participate in stacking.

### Lightning Network
Lightning enables near-instant, low-cost Bitcoin payments through payment channels. It is one of the most established Layer 2 solutions for scalability.
