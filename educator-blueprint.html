Educator Blueprint

The Educator Blueprint is a practical decision tree designed for Bitcoin educators to quickly identify their audience's needs and select the most effective teaching kits. Use the interactive questions below to get a personalized recommendation, then open the corresponding toolkit in Google Drive to copy or download the materials. The intended outcome is to make Bitcoin and Layer 2 education accessible, engaging, and effective for educators of all levels.
