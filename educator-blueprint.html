Educator Blueprint

A decision tree to help educators choose the right starting point and tools for teaching Bitcoin and Layer 2 technologies.

Onboarding Decision Tree

Step 1: Who is your audience?

  • Complete beginners / general public → Start with "1. Bitcoin as Sound Money (Beginner)"
  • Parents or students → Use "2. Self-Custody Essentials"
  • Developers or technical users → Go to "3. Practical Layer 2 Exploration"
  • Advanced users or institutions → Use "4. Advanced Sovereignty & BitVM"

Step 2: What is their current knowledge level?

  • No prior knowledge → Begin with the beginner kit + decision tree walkthrough
  • Basic understanding of Bitcoin → Jump to Layer 2 focused kits
  • Experienced with self-custody → Focus on advanced sovereignty and BitVM materials

Step 3: What is the goal?

  • Basic financial literacy → Sound Money kit
  • Self-sovereignty and security → Self-Custody kit
  • Hands-on Layer 2 experience → Practical Layer 2 kit
  • Deep technical understanding → Advanced Sovereignty kit
