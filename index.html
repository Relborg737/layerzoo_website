About This Project

LayerZoo is an educational website dedicated to exploring Bitcoin and its Layer 2 solutions through engaging stories, themed collections, and community-building resources.

The site's core mission is to educate people - especially educators - on Bitcoin and Layer 2 technologies, making it easier to onboard others into the Bitcoin ecosystem.
