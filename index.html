LayerZoo: Practical Bitcoin & Layer 2 Education for Educators and Self-Learners

LayerZoo is a practical resource hub for both Bitcoin educators and independent learners. Educators can use the interactive Educator Blueprint decision tree to assess their audience and select the most effective teaching kits. Self-learners can explore the same resources — including upcoming NFT series for each collection — to build their own understanding at their own pace. All materials are available in our shared Google Drive for easy copying or downloading. The intended outcome is to make Bitcoin and Layer 2 education accessible, engaging, and effective for everyone.
