Educator Toolkits

Modular, ready-to-deploy teaching kits that expand the Educator Blueprint decision tree. Each kit includes slide decks, worksheets, pacing guides, quizzes, and hands-on activities aligned with industry standards from Bitcoin education programs.

Integration with Blueprint: Use the decision tree on the Educator Blueprint page to identify your audience, then select the matching toolkit below.
