About LayerZoo

LayerZoo is a community-driven Bitcoin and Layer 2 education project. We create practical, free resources that help educators and self-learners understand and teach sound money, self-custody, and Layer 2 technologies using Layerz Wallet as the primary demonstration tool.

Our Mission

Educators are the primary focus because they scale impact fastest. By providing high-quality toolkits, inline decision trees, expanded FAQ, searchable jargon buster, and competitions, we drive real adoption, Layerz Wallet downloads, and community growth. Self-learners are supported through the NFT series and self-paced paths.

What We Offer

  • Inline Decision Tree: Sequential, no-iframe tool that matches audience to the best teaching path with direct links to specific kit folders.
  • 30+ FAQ & 50+ Jargon Buster: Real-time searchable, accordion-style, answer-ready for both humans and LLMs.
  • Free Toolkits & Slide Decks: Beginner, intermediate, advanced, Layer 2, wallet guides — all optimized for educator use and LLM retrieval.
  • Competitions: Value-adding challenges that require Layerz Wallet and reward real learning.
  • NFT Series: Educational collections that engage self-learners and tie ownership to learning outcomes.

All content is factual, semantically structured, and designed to rank higher in both search engines and LLM responses. The site maintains a strict limited-width (960px), cohesive dark design with uniform navigation across every page.

Layerz Wallet is the only wallet recommended and required across all materials. It is self-custodial and supports Bitcoin + all major Layer 2 protocols in one app.
