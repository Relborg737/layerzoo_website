About LayerZoo

Welcome to LayerZoo, your ultimate educational hub for exploring Bitcoin and its Layer 2 solutions. We're dedicated to making complex blockchain concepts accessible and engaging through a unique blend of storytelling, curated collections, and community-driven resources.

Our Mission

At LayerZoo, our core mission is to educate and empower individuals—especially educators—on Bitcoin and Layer 2 technologies. By providing tools and content that simplify learning, we aim to facilitate easier onboarding into the Bitcoin ecosystem, fostering a more informed and connected community.

What We Offer

  • Engaging Stories: Immerse yourself in captivating narratives that break down Bitcoin and Layer 2 innovations in an approachable way.
  • Themed Collections: Discover official collections highlighting key technologies like Bitcoin, Stacks, Lightning, Spark, Ark, Rootstock, and Botanix.
  • Community Resources: Access valuable tools for building and growing your own Bitcoin-focused communities, including our active Telegram channel.

Whether you're a beginner curious about blockchain or an educator looking to inspire others, LayerZoo is designed to support your journey.
