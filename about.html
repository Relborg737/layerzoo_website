About LayerZoo

LayerZoo is an open-source community project dedicated to Bitcoin education. It was created to help educators teach Bitcoin and Layer 2 technologies effectively while providing self-learners with high-quality resources. The ultimate goal is to grow a community around Layerz Wallet by making education the primary driver of adoption.

Our Mission

To make Bitcoin and Layer 2 education accessible to everyone through practical toolkits, decision trees, competitions, and community contributions. We believe education is the most effective way to drive long-term adoption and Layerz Wallet downloads.

How to Get Involved

Join our Telegram group, contribute to the GitHub repository, or participate in the competitions. Educators are especially welcome to submit their own lesson plans and toolkits.
